Recent Publications

A Framework for Robust GPU Management in Real-Time Systems

J. Bakita, et al.

Published in RTSS 2023

Problem Statement
Managing GPU resources for time-critical tasks is challenging, as standard schedulers are not designed for real-time guarantees.
Core Contribution
Introduced a novel scheduling framework that provides predictable performance for concurrent GPU applications, preventing deadline misses.
Potential Impact
Enables the use of powerful GPUs in safety-critical domains like autonomous vehicles and medical robotics.

Neural BRDFs: Unlocking New Frontiers in Photorealistic Rendering

L. Yan, et al.

Published in SIGGRAPH 2024

Problem Statement
Accurately modeling how light interacts with complex materials is computationally expensive, limiting realism in real-time graphics.
Core Contribution
Developed a neural network-based model (Neural BRDF) that learns material properties from real-world data and renders them with high fidelity at a fraction of the cost.
Potential Impact
Revolutionizes real-time rendering in gaming, virtual reality, and digital design by making photorealism more accessible.
